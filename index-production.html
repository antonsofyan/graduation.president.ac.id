How to Register

Here are the steps to register yourself for the graduation ceremony 2024

  1. Complete all your requirements from the Academic Bureau (all the subjects, internship, and thesis/final project are completed with minimum 144 credits).
  2. Request judicium clearance on PUIS before September 30th, 2024. More info please contact relevant department.
    • Admission: admission.requirement@president.ac.id
    • Study Program: contact study program
    • Academic: academic@president.ac.id
    • English Proficiency: ellc@president.ac.id
  3. Request Graduation Enrollment on PUIS before 03 October 2024 and fill out the exit survey. For more information contact: graduation@president.ac.id
  4. Submit 1 photo for the graduation yearbook. See the yearbook photo guidelines here.
  5. Fill out the graduation registration on PUIS before November 13th, 2024.
  6. Transfer the graduation fee of IDR 3.500.000,- for Bachelor Degree and IDR 3.750.000,- for Master Degree through the virtual account before 05 October 2024.
    More info please contact Finance (finance@president.ac.id).
  7. The Graduation fee includes:
    • 1 invitation, valid entry for 3 persons (graduate + 2 parents/relatives)
    • 3 snack packages
    • 1 set of toga
    • 1 diploma
    • 1 transcript of academic records
    • 1 photos of Graduation Ceremony moments
    • 1 souvenir
    • 1 yearbook.
  8. Finally, you have registered.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will the graduation ceremony be held?

    The 20th President University Graduation Ceremony in 2024 will be held on Sunday, 08 December 2024, offline at the President University Convention Center (PUCC), Jababeka, Cikarang, Bekasi.

  • What time will the graduation ceremony start?

    The graduation ceremony will be divided into two: morning session (starts around 8:30) and noon session (starts around 13:30). One session is allocated for two faculties. Further details on session time and session allocation will be updated on this website in time.

  • Should I attend the rehearsal?

    It is highly recommended for you to attend the rehearsal.

  • I am a Cumlaude achiever, where can I get my medal?

    Your medal will be distributed along with your academic diploma (ijazah). Please contact Academic Administration Bureau for further information.

  • I am a non-academic achiever, where can I get my medal?

    Your medal will be distributed soon. Please contact Mr. Susilo at Student Affairs Bureau for further information.

  • Do I have to show my invitation card at the graduation venue?

    Yes, you do. Your invitation is valid for two parents/relatives. You must bring your invitation card to the venue and the parents/relatives are not allowed to enter the venue without the invitation.

  • My relatives also want to join the graduation, can we request for an extra invitation card?

    The committee doesn’t provide any extra invitation card due to the limitation of the graduation venue.

  • Is there any dress code for the graduation ceremony?

    As the graduation ceremony is a formal event, you must wear formal attire, batik, or kebaya (for female) with the graduation gown (toga).

  • I cannot pickup my graduation kit, what should I do?

    You may ask your representative to take the gown (toga) for you. Taking invitations and gowns can be represented by attaching a printed power of attorney with a stamp duty. Please attach the photocopy of the representative’s ID card.

    You may use your own format of power of attorney.

  • When and where can I get my graduation kit?

    You may get the gown (toga) starting from December 02, 2024 to December 06, 2024, at President University Building A, room A211.

    The exact time and venue will be informed in time

  • Should I wear my graduation gown (toga) during the graduation?

    Yes, all graduands have to wear the graduation gown (toga) during the graduation at the venue.

  • Should we return the graduation gown/toga after the ceremony?

    No, it’s all yours.

  • I couldn’t register for the graduation last year, can I register for the graduation this year?

    Sure, please go to your PUIS for registration.

  • What does the graduation fee cover?

    The graduation fee covers the graduation kit (gown/toga, yearbook), diploma/ijazah, graduation production.
